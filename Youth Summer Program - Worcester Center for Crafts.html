Welcome to the 2025 Youth Summer Program at Worcester Center for Crafts

Unlock Creativity, Spark Imagination, and Craft Memories this Summer!

Are you looking for an enriching and fun-filled summer experience for your child? Look no further! Worcester Center for Crafts is thrilled to present our 2025 Youth Summer Program.

About the Program

Duration: 8 Weeks of Creative Exploration
Ages: WPI Students aged 18-21
Schedule: Full-day workshops from 9:00 am – 3:00 pm
Pricing: Starts at $240

Discover the Magic of Crafting

Our program is designed to ignite your child’s imagination through a diverse range of craft activities. From pottery to painting, jewelry-making to woodworking, your child will embark on a journey of artistic discovery.

For Ages 7 - 9

Youth Summer Program Week 1: Ecosystems! | June 30th - July 3rd

Price: $320

Morning: Ecosystem Abstract creations in Stained Glass

Create a mosaic-like stained glass window hanging using multiple pieces of colored glass chosen from bins of pre-cut smaller pieces of glass. Add glass nuggets or other small solderable shapes to create a one-of-a-kind abstract window hanging. No experience is necessary, all materials are included.

Please wear natural fiber fabrics, long pants, and closed-toe shoes.

**Please bring lunch & snacks for this all-day course.**

Afternoon: Ecosystem Art Experiments with Found Objects
Lets go on a hunt to find things that we can make into art! Did you know you can make drawings, paintings, or prints by combining things from around the house and nature? Its true! Artists have used found objects in artmaking for a long time, and we will check out their work and then go on a scavenger hunt to find fun materials! We will even turn those found objects into 3D sculptures, collages, and assemblages. Try new ways to look at things as art and experiment with creatively putting objects together. Go home with all kinds of art and art tools that you made from things that you found.

Students will do a scavenger hunt at home to gather materials to bring in.

Youth Summer Program Week 2: Geometric Art | July 7th - 11th

Price: $400

Morning & Afternoon: Geometric Stained Glass

Join us for a hands-on stained glass workshop where youll create your own unique designs inspired by geometric patterns! In this class, youll learn how to cut, arrange, and assemble colorful glass shapes to form bold, eye-catching artwork. No experience is necessary; all materials are included.

Please wear natural fiber fabrics, long pants, and closed-toe shoes.

***Please bring lunch & snacks for this all-day course.***

Youth Summer Program Week 3: Botanical Explorations | July 14th - 16th

Price: $240

Morning & Afternoon: Botanical Explorations with Metal Tap into the unique properties of copper, brass, and aluminum in this brand-new program for young artists! We will use foil, mesh, and wire to create inspired sculptures, decorations, and simple jewelry. Learn to envision your ideas and apply new tools and techniques to bring them to life. A great foundation for further exploration of the wonderful world of metal! Please wear natural fiber fabrics, long pants, and closed-toe shoes.

**Please bring lunch & snacks for this all-day course.**

Youth Summer Program Week 4: Forest Fantasy | July 21st - 25th

Price: $400

Morning & Afternoon: Forged in Fantasy

Interested in learning to make your own jewelry? Join us for a week of fire and fantasy! In this course, students will learn basic metalsmithing skills, including but not limited to how to use a jewelers saw, how to solder pieces of metal together using a torch, and how to clean and polish their pieces. They will explore the concept of fantasy and imagination through magical creatures that will be used to inspire their pieces. Students will be able to create rings, pendants, pins, and much more!

***Please bring lunch & snacks for this all-day course.***

SIGN UP

Youth Summer Program Week 5: Fables | July 28th - August 1st

Price: $400

Morning & Afternoon: Stitching Enchanted Creatures!

Students will spend time learning basic sewing techniques, including threading a needle, tying knots, and mastering stitches like the running and blanket stitches. They will then work on projects such as making an owl pincushion, sewing a simple stuffed animal from a premade pattern, and designing their own stuffed animal pattern.

***Please bring lunch & snacks for this all-day course.***

SIGN UP

Youth Summer Program Week 6 & 7: Timeless Tales | August 4th - 15th

Price: $700

Morning & Afternoon: Vaudeville Mysteries

In this two-week class, we will be working as a creative team to produce an artistic performance with a playful Vaudevillian theme. Together, we will create the props, backdrops, and characters for our performance, as well as strengthen our theatre skills with warm-up games each class. The end collaboration will be shaped using the strengths of each student and will involve the opportunity for each individuals ideas as well. In the short performance at the end of the two weeks, we will find out who or what your character is and what they left in the mysterious Avalon.

***Please bring lunch & snacks for this all-day course.***

SIGN UP

Youth Summer Program Week 8: Jungle Adventure | August 18th - 22nd

Price: $400

Morning & Afternoon: Piatas!!!

Each student will create their own Piata from start to finish! In this one-week course, we will start with the design process, exploring the best techniques and tools to make your ideas come to life. Next, we will create an armature or understructure out of cardboard for our design, which will then be decorated with a variety of materials of each students choosing. Filling the Piata and breaking it open will be optional! No experience is necessary; all materials are included.

 

***Please bring lunch & snacks for this all-day course.***

For Ages 10 - 13

Youth Summer Program Week 1: Ecosystems! | June 30th - July 3rd

Price: $320

Morning & Afternoon: Ecosystem Fashionable Forging

Design and hammer into shape your own copper jewelry! Well start with stylish pendants you can string on linen cord and wear immediately. Then, well move on to crafting wire cuff bracelets, followed by wider bracelets forged from copper bars. Hammer, bend, twist, texture, rivet, and solder your way to a unique fashion statement! Please wear natural fiber fabrics, long pants, and closed-toe shoes.

Please wear natural fiber fabrics, long pants, and closed-toe shoes.

Youth Summer Program Week 2: Geometric Art | July 7th - 11th

Price: $400

Morning: Geometry Chainmail Jewelry and Keychains

In this course, students will learn the basics of chainmail construction. Chainmail, historically used as armor, is made by interlocking small metal rings to form chains and sheets in different patterns. Using two pairs of pliers to manipulate colorful anodized aluminum rings, students will design and construct their own chainmail bracelets, keychains, and more!

Please wear natural fiber fabrics, long pants, and closed-toe shoes.

***Please bring lunch & snacks for this all-day course.***

Afternoon: Paper Possibilities

Learn about all of the possibilities for paper. Make your own paper from recycled paper and plant fibers. Then, try creative ways to attach and bind paper into your paper into your own handmade journal. Explore ways to use paper pulp as paint, like clay, and create paper mache sculptures.

Hold Everything — or Nothing! Heres a fun exercise! Think of things youd like to collect and keep–even if its only air or memories–and envision containers to hold them.
